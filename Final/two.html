Curacao is a beautiful island and has many great attractions for tourist. Persoanlly, I’sm a big beach person and this country had some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. The sand was beautiful and soft and the water was warm to swim in. I spent most of my days tanning away at the beach and was relaxing all around. The photo to the left is me at one of the beaches on this beautiful island and as you can see it has some beautiful water that is lovely to swim in.










One of the more eventful things that happen here was we went on a ATV tour across the island. This was my first time riding a ATV and let me tell you, it will get you adrenaline running. The tour guide had the most amazing persoanlity and brought us to the most amazing places. We got to see this amazing ocean side cliff, went into a cave, and went to the top of moutain that had the most amazing view. This photo to the right is a picture of me and my cousin riding in the ATV through the forest. It was so fun and a expereince I will cheriese forever, find out more at ATV buggy tours
